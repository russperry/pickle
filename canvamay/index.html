13Decisions for this room · A

Which creative direction do we run?

Three concepts illustrated at /sample — Balanced, Aggressive, Elegant. Or a hybrid. The deck doesn't pre-pick. The team picks today.

Option 01 · Balanced
Trust-forward, enterprise-credible, broad appeal.
Closest to current DP voice. Easiest internal sell. Lowest creative risk.
Option 02 · Aggressive
Tongue-in-cheek pain points. Names the audience in the headline.
"Senior designer ≠ Canva tech support." Highest paid-media performance, highest narrative leverage. Riskier inside Canva's brand norms.
Option 03 · Elegant
Quiet authority, design-first, premium feel.
Best fit for enterprise migration story. Lower distinctiveness in paid. Strongest for Canva exec audience.

15Decisions for this room · C

How does a DP AE introduce themselves to a Canva AE?

This is the operational heart. Jen's bridge motion only works if every DP AE is trained to do the AE-to-AE handshake without coaching. Today we lock the script, the medium, and the cadence.

The Script
What's the one-paragraph intro every DP AE sends?
No HubSpot deal-reg form. No marketing speak. Names a specific Canva account. Offers a specific assist.
The Medium
Slack, LinkedIn, email — and in what sequence?
Recommendation: warm-channel-first (LinkedIn → DM), email as fallback, never form.
The Cadence
Wave outreach or rolling? Paired with whom?
Recommendation: waves of ~10 reps/week, paired Jen + DP AE, Austin first.
16The anti-pattern slide

What we're not doing.

As important as what we are. Every "no" below was either tried, considered, or recommended — and would burn us.

We are doing

  • Bullet-point deal categories first
  • Canva AE microsite — one page, no friction
  • Organic + AE motion (we outrank Canva on its own terms)
  • Tight ICP wedge with overwhelming proof
  • Two parallel audiences — public demand gen + internal AEs

We are not

  • Locking contract terms or commission splits
  • Sending a HubSpot deal-reg form to Canva AEs
  • Bidding on Canva keywords on our own dime
  • Going generic — "creative services for everyone"
  • Going exclusive on Canva (yet)
